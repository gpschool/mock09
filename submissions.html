The workshop will be single-day, comprising of a short introduction, invited talks (20 mins) and presentations of contributed work, with time for discussions. Presentations of contributed work consist of brief talks and spotlights.

Contributions should be communicated in form of an extended abstract (up to 4 pages), using the NIPS style template. Contributions should be sent to nips.mock09 A T gmail. com
